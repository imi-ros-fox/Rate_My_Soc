About Rate My Society

A platform for students to discover, review, and rate university societies.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help students make informed decisions when joining societies. We provide honest reviews and ratings from real students.

What You Can Do

  • Browse and search societies
  • Leave ratings and reviews
  • Upvote helpful reviews
  • Create and manage societies (for presidents)
